Hello, I'm Tony

I'm a driven high school student with an insatiable curiosity for understanding both the digital realm and the natural world. Through music, I've discovered a powerful medium for creative expression and connection. Whether I'm coding algorithms, analyzing geological formations, or performing with my instrument, I approach each challenge with the same dedication and enthusiasm.
